Hmong Wedding

Hmong weddings are a vibrant and culturally rich celebration that showcases the traditions and customs of the Hmong people. These weddings are often characterized by colorful attire and meaningful rituals that reflect the deep-rooted values of family and community. Hmong weddings are a joyous occasion that brings together loved ones to celebrate love and unity.

Cov Zaj

Niam Txiv Hauv Tsev cob Kaus

Hais!!! Zoo li kus oos, zoo li kus, zoo li txhiab niam mus lawm txhiab xyoo, hmoob pog tub hlob tsis tau tiav txuj, hmoob pog tub yuav tsis zoo los mus phwb tshoob, hmoob poj tub yuav rau siab ntsuj ntso mus leg lawm hmoob pog lub mus luaj qoob yuas

Zoo li txiab niam mus lawm txhiab xyoo, hmoob yawg tub hlub tsis tau los mus tiav ci, hmoob yawg tub yuav tsis zoo los mus phwb kos, hmoob yawg tub yuav rau siab ntsuj ntso mus leg lawm hmoob yawg lub mus luaj qoob yuas

Zoo li xyoo no es hmoob pog tub hlub los mus tia txuj, hmoob pog tub yuav zoo los mus phwb tshoob, hmoob pog tub yuav tsis xav mus leg hmoob pog lub mus luaj qoob yuas

Zoo li xyoo no es hmoob yawg tub hlub los mus tia ci, hmoob yawg tub yuav zoo los mus phwb kos, hmoob yawg tub yuav tsis xav mus leg lawm hmoob yawg lub mus luaj nrog yuas

Zoo li hwm cwb raug tsis muaj yua, txiv tuam mej koob lwm mej zeeg es, neb nyob lawm neb cuaj hnub kev kuj mus sis zog, kuv niam kuv txiv muab lub xov tooj hu li zog tuaj thov lawm neb lub zog txog

Zoo li hwm cwb raug tsis muaj yua, txiv tuam mej koob lwm mej zeeg es, neb nyob lawm neb cuaj hmo kev kuj mus sis zag, kuv niam kuv txiv muab lub xov tooj hu li zog tuaj thov lawm neb lub zog tag

Zoo li neb ncig li yeev tuaj txog, kuv niam kuv txiv rub rooj li zog rau neb nyob es, tsab kab luam yeeb nplooj ntsuab rau neb xo, kuv nima tsuas zoo siab, nws ntes lauj ntho li nkaus nkawm puj qaib los tuarau neb noj, nws plaj tshoob kev kos yuav tso rau neb mus nrog coj, zoo li noj los neb tsis tau ib dia mus zoo noj, haus los neb tsis tau ib khob mus zoo haus, kuv niam kuv txiv tes lauj mus txo niam nkauj kaus txiv pwj li nyug los cob li nkaus rau neb tej mus quav npab, neb yuav muab neb niam nkauj kaus roos neb nkauj nyab tub ntsig laj mus ntoj Kab

Zoo li neb ncig li yeev tuaj txog, kuv niam kuv txiv rub rooj li zog rau neb zaum es, tsab kab luam yeeb paj dawb rau neb haus, kuv txiv tsuas zoo plawv, nws txo hwj cawv los rau neb haus, nws plaj tshoob kev kos yuav tso rau neb mus nrog taug, zoo li noj los neb tsis tau ib dia mus zoo noj, haus los neb tsis tau ib khob mus zoo haus, kuv niam kuv txiv tes lauj mus txo niam nkauj kaus seev pob seev sis nyas los cob li nkaus rau neb tej mus quav tes, neb yuav muab neb niam nkauj kaus roos neb nkauj nyab tub ntsig laj mus ntoj kev

Zoo li neb tawm rooj li plaws, neb yuav yoj kaus li yeev mus txog tej qab roob, neb yuav tsom li nraim kuv niam kuv txiv txoj mus kab tshoob
